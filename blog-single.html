Putta Mahesh Kumar: A Visionary Leader and Champion of Progress

Putta Mahesh Kumar 12 Jun 2024
7,46,351 Votes 1,81,857 Majority

An Young Energetic Politician who has been elected by the people of Andhra Pradesh as a Member of Parliament (M.P) for the district of Eluru in the general election of 2024.

He secured a landslide victory in the Member of Parliament (Lok Sabha) elections, garnering an unprecedented majority of 1,81,857 votes. This achievement set a new record for the highest majority in the history of the Eluru parliament sector. Additionally, he made history as one of the youngest politicians to be elected as an MP candidate from the Telugu Desam Party in the general elections of 2024.

Driven by a strong sense of humanitarianism, he firmly advocates for the active participation of everyone in creating a better world for the less privileged. Leading by example, he has adopted his ancestral village in the Mydukur District of Andhra Pradesh. Through his philanthropic efforts, he has undertaken the construction of schools, provision of drinking water facilities, and improvement of infrastructure.

Additionally, he has been actively involved in storm and flood relief activities, along with various other charitable initiatives through his own charitable Trust, named "Putta Vijayalakshmi Seva Society," aiming to foster a more equitable, humane, and sustainable society.

Mahesh Kumar's commitment extends beyond business and politics to impactful social work. His transformative initiatives in Kothapalli, ensuring essential amenities like clean drinking water, well-maintained roads, and proper street lighting, reflect his genuine concern for community welfare. As a dynamic and forward-thinking leader, Putta Mahesh Kumar is dedicated to fostering sustainable development, promoting innovation, and enhancing the quality of life for his constituents. Early Life and Education

Putta Mahesh Kumar was born to Smt. Putta Vijayalakshmi and Putta Sudhakar Yadav in Proddutur, Kadapa district. His father is a member of Telugu Desam Party and a member of Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly from Mydukur Assembly constituency. He was the former chairman of the Board of Trustees of Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams.
