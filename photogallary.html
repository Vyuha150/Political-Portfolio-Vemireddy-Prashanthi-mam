Portfolio of Activities – Vemireddy Prabhakar Reddy

  1. Political Leadership & Governance
    Elected Member of Parliament (Nellore), Member, Committee on Public Undertakings
    Demonstrates active legislative engagement, overseeing public sector enterprises and contributing to policy discussions at the national level.
  2. Organizing Employment Opportunities
    Event: Mega Job Fair, Nellore | Organizer: VPR Foundation
    Connected job seekers with employers and skill resources. Hundreds registered for interviews; new campus-to-corporate linkages established.
  3. Disaster Relief & Philanthropy
    Donation to Chief Minister’s Flood Relief Fund (Sep 3, 2024)
    Mobilized relief materials and supported rehabilitation for flood-affected families.
  4. Ration Supply Distribution
    Venue: 102nd Ration Depot, Nellore | Collaborator: Minister Narayana
    Thousands of ration kits distributed to underprivileged households, strengthening food security.
  5. Ration Card Enrollment Services
    Six new services launched for applications, updates, and corrections. 40% increase in timely registrations.
  6. Party Convention Participation
    Event: TDP MahaNadu 2025 | #మహానాడు2025
    Key participant and speaker, focusing on rural development, employment, and grassroots outreach.

  8. Additional Humanitarian Contribution
    Donated funds and distributed essentials to over 500 flood-affected families, with ongoing rehabilitation monitoring.
