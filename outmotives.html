Aim


As a dedicated Member of Parliament for Eluru, Putta Mahesh Kumar's primary aim is to foster sustainable development and enhance the quality of life for all citizens in his constituency. His vision encompasses comprehensive socio-economic growth, prioritizing the welfare of farmers, youth empowerment, infrastructure development, and the promotion of cultural heritage.
